    Services
    We proudly serve individuals of all ages who have been diagnosed with an intellectual or developmental disability (IDD), including but not limited to:
  • Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD)
  • Down Syndrome
  • Cerebral Palsy
  • Intellectual Disability
  • Other qualifying developmental conditions

    • To be eligible for services, individuals must:
  • Reside in or plan to move to the Chicagoland area
  • Have a documented IDD diagnosis
    • Community Living (CILA)

    24/7 support in safe, luxurious homes that promote independence and dignity in a family-like environment.

    Assistance with ADLs

    Support with hygiene, meals, dressing, and mobility — delivered with dignity and care tailored to each individual.

    Life Skills & Vocational Training

    Daily living skills and job-readiness programs that empower individuals with confidence and independence.

    Community Integration

    Social outings, events, and community involvement that build connection and inclusion in real-world settings.

    Family & Respite Support

    Short-term care and guidance for families and caregivers — offering relief, education, and support resources.
