    About Us
    Our Story
    Over the years, we've grown into a compassionate team of direct support professionals, advocates, case managers, and community partners who work hand-in-hand with individuals and their families. We provide person-centered services that go beyond daily support—we empower people to build skills, nurture relationships, find their voice, and create a future they envision for themselves.

    Through residential programs, day services, job readiness training, and community engagement initiatives, we help individuals with I/DD live with dignity, safety, and purpose. Our approach is grounded in respect, inclusivity, and the belief that true care means meeting people where they are—supporting them not just in tasks, but in thriving.

    Every step we take is guided by the voices and choices of the individuals we serve. Together, we are building a community where everyone belongs.

    Today, The Royal Family Corporation serves families across Chicagoland with a model that blends expert support, beautiful living spaces, and an unshakable belief in every individual's worth. Because in this family, everyone matters — and everyone belongs.
    Our Mission
    To enrich the lives of members promoting LAVISH living providing compassionate, community-based living and personalized support. Our mission is to foster a safe, inclusive environment where each person can develop independence, build meaningful relationships, and reach their full potential. Through our commitment to dignity, respect, and empowerment, we aim to create a person-centered community that celebrates the unique abilities and aspirations of every individual we serve.
    Our Vision
    T.R.F.C vision is to build a world where individuals with unique abilities are fully integrated, respected, and empowered to live their lives to the fullest. We strive to be a model of inclusive community living, where every member feels valued, has opportunities for personal growth, and can contribute meaningfully to society. Through our dedication, compassionate care and community connection, we envision a future where barriers are removed, potential is realized, and all individuals are embraced as vital members of the community all while Living LAVISH.
    Why "The Royal Family"?
    The Royal Family Cooperation is a nonprofit organization dedicated to creating safe, inclusive, and supportive living environments for individuals with unique abilities through CILA services. Our mission is to empower each member to live LAVISH with dignity, independence, and a sense of belonging.

    We believe that every person deserves a space where they are valued, understood, and encouraged to pursue their dreams.

    Our Commitment to Care

    Who We Serve

    The individuals we serve in our CILA programs are adults (18 years and older) with intellectual, developmental, or physical disabilities — individuals with what we proudly call unique abilities. Each person is welcomed into a home-like setting where their independence, goals, and self-expression are honored through tailored, person-centered care.

    Our Philosophy

    We believe in more than care — we believe in dignity. Our philosophy is rooted in person-centered service that promotes respect, empowerment, and individualized support. From staff training to day-to-day interactions, our culture is designed to uplift every member's voice and choice.

    What We Offer

    The Royal Family Corporation provides specialized, person-centered support for individuals with unique abilities and diverse needs. Our services are built to empower members to live with confidence, purpose, and independence — all while nurturing a true sense of family, community, and belonging.
