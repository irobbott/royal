    About Us
    Our Story
    The Royal Family Corporation began with a simple but powerful belief — that every person, regardless of ability, deserves to be treated like royalty.

    Our founder, Edwina Smith, has spent over 20 years serving individuals with intellectual and developmental disabilities (IDD) as a Direct Support Professional. Throughout her career, she witnessed both the beauty and the brokenness of the care system: dedicated families stretched thin, individuals living without dignity, and services that fell short of truly honoring those they served.

    Driven by her faith, compassion, and deep understanding of the gaps in quality care, Edwina created The Royal Family Corporation to be different — to be personal, luxurious, and community-rooted. This work isn't just professional for her — it's personal. It's about restoring humanity to the way we care.

    Today, The Royal Family Corporation serves families across Chicagoland with a model that blends expert support, beautiful living spaces, and an unshakable belief in every individual's worth. Because in this family, everyone matters — and everyone belongs.
    Our Mission
    We believe every person deserves more than support — they deserve to be seen, valued, and celebrated. That's why we provide community-integrated services that are as inclusive as they are luxurious. Our team of dedicated professionals delivers personalized care in safe, beautiful environments where individuals with IDD can thrive, and families can feel peace of mind. This is not just care. It's royal care — for every life we touch.

    Why "The Royal Family"?
    At The Royal Family Corporation, the name is more than a title — it's a promise.

    We believe that every individual deserves to be treated with dignity, respect, and care worthy of royalty. Our clients aren't just service recipients; they're family. And like any true family, we center love, comfort, and belonging in everything we do.

    From the way we design our living spaces to how we build care plans and community connections, we approach every detail with the intention of making our clients feel honored, valued, and at home — because that's what royalty deserves.
